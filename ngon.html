    *How to Calculate its Area*

    Any Regular N-gon

    Objective: Given its apothem and perimeter, find the area of a Regular N-gon.

    Given Values: a = 5; p = 36
    Step Explanation Work
    1 Substitute 'a' for '5' and 'p' for '36' into the formula, '1/2ap'.
  • A = 1/2ap
  • = 1/2(5)(36)
    		•
    2 Multiply '1/2' times '36'.
  • A = 1/2(5)(36)
  • = (18)(5)
    		•
    3 Multiply '18' times '5' to get the area of the Regular N-gon.
  • A = (18)(5)
  • = 90 units2
