    *How to Calculate its Area*

    Ellipse

    Objective: Given radius1 and radius2, find the area of an Ellipse.

    Given Values: a = 4; b = 7
    Step Explanation Work
    1 Substitute 'a' for '4' and 'b' for '7' into the formula, 'πab'.
  • A = πab
  • = π(4)(7)
    		•
    2 Multiply '4' times '7'.
  • A = π(4)(7)
  • = π(28)
    		•
    3 Multiply '28' times 'π' to get the area of the Ellipse.
  • A = π(28)
  • ≈ 87.96 units2
