    *How to Calculate its Area*

    Triangle

    Objective: Given its base and height, find the area of a Triangle.

    Given Values: b = 9; h = 5
    Step Explanation Work
    1 Substitute 'b' for '9' and 'h' for '5' into the formula, '1/2bh'.
  • A = 1/2bh
  • = 1/2(9)(5)
    		•
    2 Multiply '1/2' times '9'.
  • A = 1/2(9)(5)
  • = (4.5)(5)
    		•
    3 Multiply '4.5' times '5' to get the area of the Triangle.
  • A = (4.5)(5)
  • = 22.5 units2
