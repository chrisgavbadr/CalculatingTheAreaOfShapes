    *How to Calculate its Volume*

    Pyramid

    Objective: Given its length, width, and height, find the volume of a Pyramid.

    Given Values: l = 5; w = 6; h = 7
    Step Explanation Work
    1 Substitute 'l' for '5', 'w' for '6', and 'h' for '7' into the formula, 'lwh'.
  • V = lwh/3
  • = (5)(6)(7)/3
    		•
    2 Multiply '5' times '6'.
  • V = (5)(6)(7)/3
  • = (30)(7)/3
    		•
    3 Multiply '30' times '7'.
  • V = (30)(7)/3
  • = (210)/3
    		•
    4 Divide '210' by '3' to get the volume of the Pyramid.
  • V = (210)/3
  • = 70 units3
